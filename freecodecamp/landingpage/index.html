Features
  • We provide media to make easy to make test pages.
  • We care of media and you care of styling.
  • We offer a lot of media types:
    • Text: forget meaningless Lorem Ipsum, we offer a lot of meaningful texts, with or without HTML markup.
    • Images: tired of looking for public domain images, we have lots of them in many different sizes.
    • Audio: Do you need some readed text, music or sound effects? This is the place you're looking for.
    • Video: You can get lot's of videos premade for you with text tracks in different languages or by selecting audio and soundless animations that we'll combine.
  • All of our media is of public domain, so you won't have any problem using it in your tests.
  • All of our work is open source, licensed under mit license, check us on GitHub.
